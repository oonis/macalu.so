---
title: Flighty
description: Flight tracking app
---

[Official Page](https://www.flightyapp.com/)

The best flight tracking app I've used. It pulls data from multiple sources and will often know about delays and gate changes before the airline's own app does. I've had a few trips where I would have been stuck at the wrong gate if not for Flighty catching a last minute change.

The UI gives you a clean timeline of your flight with everything you'd want at a glance like gate, terminal, baggage carousel, and seat info. You can also see where your actual plane is coming from which gives you a real sense of whether your flight will be delayed before it's officially announced.

It's a premium app with a subscription but if you fly more than a few times a year it's well worth it. The free tier still gives you solid tracking for a single flight at a time.
