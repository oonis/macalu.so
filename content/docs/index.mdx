Hey, I'm Sam — a software developer who researches way too much into mundane things.

This site is my second brain: a place where I document the things I've found, use, and recommend. Think of it as a personal wiki that I maintain for myself, but figured others might find useful too.

## What's here

- [Best in Slot](/best-in-slot) — My curated picks for everyday items, from office gear to kitchen essentials. I'm not an influencer, just someone with questionable spending habits and a tendency to over-research everything.

## About this site

Built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) and [Fumadocs](https://fumadocs.vercel.app), deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com). The source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/oonis/macalu.so/) if you're curious.

Feel free to poke around.
