Hey, I'm Sam — a software developer who researches way too much into mundane things.

This site is my second brain: a place where I document the things I've found, use, and recommend. Think of it as a personal wiki that I maintain for myself, but figured others might find useful too.

## What's here

### [Best in Slot](/best-in-slot)

My curated picks for everyday items, from office gear to kitchen essentials. I'm not an influencer, just someone with questionable spending habits and a tendency to over-research everything.

- [Office](/best-in-slot/office) — Desk, chair, speakers, and laptop
- [Kitchen](/best-in-slot/kitchen) — Rice cooker, trash can, and other essentials
- [Living Room](/best-in-slot/living-room) — TV, console, and chore helpers
- [Subscriptions](/best-in-slot/subscriptions) — Password manager, journaling, and more
